What is 1st Pic ?

1st Pic is a free photo services app that matches freelance photographers with potential clients.

Whether you need a well tailored video for your company’s holiday party, product shots for that clothing line you've been working on, or if you just want to see the best version of yourself in front of a camera, 1st Pic will get you there in a snap. So go ahead, be the 1st to Pic to your vision.
